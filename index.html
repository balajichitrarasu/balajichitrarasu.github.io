About Balaji Chitrarasu

I am a Hardware & Software Technical Engineer from India — ECE graduate from United Institute of Technology. I have deep hands-on experience in desktop and laptop repair, OS installation, BIOS flashing, SSD/HDD servicing, battery replacement, and full system diagnostics.

I have a strong passion for electronics — I love dismantling any device to understand how every component works at a part level. Give me any electronic device and I will figure out how it works from the inside.

I am a SEBI-certified Stock Market Trader and have built my own automated trading system called TradingBoy — designed to analyse market patterns and execute trades based on technical indicators.

I serve as a MoE Institution Innovation Council (IIC) Student Innovation Ambassador — certified at Foundation, Advanced, and Re-skilling levels with 67+ hours of innovation training.

Currently pursuing AWS Cloud Certification. I am also a HackerRank certified Software Engineer and hold 7 HP LIFE certificates in AI, Data Science, Cybersecurity, Leadership, and more.

I believe in learning by doing — whether it is building a PC from scratch, setting up an office network, or writing a trading algorithm. Always learning, always building.
