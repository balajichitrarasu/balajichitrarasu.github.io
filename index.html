About Me

I am a Hardware & Software Technical Engineer from India (ECE — United Institute of Technology) with deep hands-on experience in desktop and laptop repair, OS installation, BIOS flashing, SSD/HDD servicing, battery replacement, and full system diagnostics.

I have a strong passion for electronics — I love dismantling any device to understand how every component works. I am a certified SEBI Stock Market Trader and have built my own automated trading system called TradingBoy.

I serve as a MoE Institution Innovation Council (IIC) Student Innovation Ambassador — certified at Foundation, Advanced and Re-skilling levels. Currently pursuing AWS Cloud Certification. HackerRank certified Software Engineer. Always learning, always building.
