About Balaji Chitrarasu

I am a Hardware & Software Technical Engineer from India — an ECE graduate of United Institute of Technology. I have deep hands-on experience in desktop and laptop repair, OS installation, BIOS flashing, SSD and HDD servicing, battery replacement and full system diagnostics.

I have a strong passion for electronics — I love understanding how every component works at part level. I am a SEBI-certified Stock Market Trader and have built my own automated trading system called TradingBoy.

I serve as a MoE IIC Student Innovation Ambassador — certified at Foundation, Advanced and Re-skilling levels with over 67 hours of innovation training under the Government of India.

Currently pursuing AWS Cloud Certification. Also a HackerRank-certified Software Engineer and hold seven HP LIFE certificates covering AI, Data Science, Cybersecurity, Leadership and Business Communication.

I believe in learning by doing — whether building a PC from scratch, setting up an office network, or writing a trading algorithm. Always learning. Always building.
